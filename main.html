hello, _____! ex•qui•site•cor•pus is an exercise in collaborative storytelling, based on an old game of a similar name. here are the rules:

0. hop on one of the channels below.
1. wait for everyone to join the same channel. when you're ready, press the "start game" button.
2. when it's your turn, type whatever you want in the textbox! (it'll only take 280 characters, though.) once you've typed your masterpiece, press the "done" button.
3. the next player does the same thing, but they won't be able to see anything the previous people have typed, save for the last word. (for example, if you typed "i do not like green eggs and ham", the player after you will only see "ham".)
4. repeat for 5 rounds.
5. at the end of the game, the screen will display your story in its entirety. try giving each other a dramatic reading!
***by the way, you'll have video, audio, and text chat capabilities the whole time :~)
